A business decision game that teaches and tests core economics concepts.

How The Game Works

You are the new strategy lead at a growing business. In each round, you will choose the best economic decision.

  1. Read the business scenario carefully.
  2. Pick the answer you think is best for the company.
  3. Get instant feedback and learn why that answer works.
  4. Finish all rounds to see your report card.

You will be tested on:

  • Principal-agent problems in organizations
  • Risk-adjusted return calculations
  • Portfolio theory in product/initiative mix decisions
  • Moral hazard in guaranteed contracts
  • Asset valuation under uncertainty
  • Capital structure decisions
  • Revenue recognition timing
